Week 6: Intellectual Property / Week 2 of HTML/CSS --> other tools & templates

Building on our basic HTML/CSS literacies from last week, we will take a look at other tools that can help us create our own content online -- we'll primarily focus on web design tools and templates built from bootstrap, but we'll briefly explore other WYSIWYG content creators. Our readings will discuss intellectual property and licensing online.

Readings for the week:

  • Lessig's Ted Talk: Laws that Choke Creativity
  • Skim: Types of open source licenses. You don't have to read all of these word-for-word, but just get a sense for the language in these licenses.
