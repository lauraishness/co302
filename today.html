Article Discussion Questions

  • First, how is the article defining a “Digital Native”? Do you consider yourself a “digital native”? What does that mean to you?
  • What are the article’s central claims?
  • Do you agree? Disagree? Is this claim still valid in some way? If so, how? If not, how?
  • Should our online identities matter? How? How do you approach your online identity? How do you (or do you) set parameters on what you post online (what platforms, privacy settings)? What’s your approach to your digital identity?
