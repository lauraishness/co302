Module 9

What will the future of writing look like?

Introduction to Module 9

This week as we return from extended spring break, we’ll regroup & look to the future. First, we’ll explore emerging technologies that challenge our notions of textuality; by that, I mean, what does “writing” mean when the tools & texts change with innovation. What does the future of communication look like if we’re no longer putting pen to page -- or even pressing keys on a keyboard? One day, will we be able to write by thinking? If we’re doing it right, this week’s discussion will go off the rails with creativity and sci-fi-like predictions.

Everything you need to complete for the course is explained and linked below. Start with the Overview to get the gist of what’s required this week.
