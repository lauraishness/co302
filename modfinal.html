For this week, we'll wrap up our course with A4's Celebrations of Research that will continue into Finals week. Your main task for the week is to continue working on A4, sign up for a presentation timeslot, and share your research with your classmates.

  1. Continue working on A4's Final Project Due on Wed. 5/13 by 11:59pm
  2. Attend class on Wednesday, Friday, or during Tuesday's final exam time to hear from your peers. Note: attendance is optional, but encouraged.
  3. If you choose, you may write an additional instigator (email to Laura) for your peers' final presentation.

Independent Research for A4

Attend your peers' presentations as you are able to (see Canvas announcements for links) to celebrate their hard work and hear about their awesome projects.

Most weeks, we’ll have two discussion forum posts. For now, I’m using Google Groups for these forum posts. (Open to other suggestions if this ends up sucking; however, this is the easiest way I found to link to content from our CO302 page while also keeping discussions private.)
