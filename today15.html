Instigator Questions

  • What challenges arise when discussing who owns an image that has been circulated over a period of time?
  • How do you think being able to manipulate photos like the Obama Hope challenges creativity and originality?
  • Why do influential images using photoshop, become more iconic than original photographs of an earlier era?
  • Does the constant transformation of meaning require constant connectivity to interpret? That is, to understand the rhetorical value of an event-image, do you have to understand the history behind the original image and its consequent transformations? If someone had never seen the original Obama Hope image and one day just stumbles across one of the spoofs, what would happen?
