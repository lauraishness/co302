Module 12

Infographic Tracking / Digital Research Methods

Introduction to Module 12

If you haven't done so yet, complete Module 9, Week 11 conference

This week, we'll look into viral phenonmenons in digital rhetoric. We'll explore Grie's article about infographic tracking to (1) find out more about visual rhetoric and (2) see some strategies for digital rhetoric research in action.

Everything you need to complete for the course is explained and linked below. Start with the Overview to get the gist of what’s required this week.
