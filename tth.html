Instigator Discussion Questions

  • How will affordances change as we transition into a virtual world with less buttons and more augmented reality and artificial intelligence?
  • What ways do you break the mold and explore the affordances of objects in your every day life?
  • How often do we as a culture actually allow for new affordances from objects? (Do we just build/buy niche products to avoid multi-uses from items we already own?)
  • If our realities are this easy to alter, moving forward how do we know what is and isn’t real?
