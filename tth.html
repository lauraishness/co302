Instigator Discussion Questions

  • How do you know what is the most important design element to be considered when creating a website? What do you think it is, and how does this relate to the needs of the humans?
  • Interfaces that are designed to influence our decision making, is that a good design or bad design? Harmful or Helpful?
  • In terms of designing a physical product, in what ways do companies or producers be sure to connect on a personable level and how does this help their design be more successful?
  • In human-centered design when is it necessary for aesthetics to give-way to the necessities and point of view of the users or clients?
