Week 10: Going Viral

We'll discuss "going viral" and the rhetorical strategies involved in what Ridolfo and DeVoss call "rhetorical velocity"; we'll also hear from a guest via podcast.

Readings for the week:

  • "Going Viral, Getting Likes" by by Daniel Wuebben (PDF, see Canvas)
  • Composing for Recomposition: Rhetorical Velocity and Delivery by Jim Ridolfo and Dànielle Nicole DeVoss
  • Chapter 3 from Digital Rhetoric: Theory, Method, Practice by Douglas Eyman
  • Also: examine a viral phenomenon of your choosing. Bring to class on Wednesday and be ready to discuss.
